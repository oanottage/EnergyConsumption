Energy Consumption Prediction - By Onassis Nottage

Course: Big Data Infrastructure


The goal of this project is to predict energy consumption based on various features such as temperature, humidity, time of day, etc. The two sources of data for this project could be energy consumption data and weather data. You can get the energy consumption data from the US Energy Information Administration (https://www.eia.gov/opendata/qb.php?category=2122628) and the weather data from NOAA (https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/cdo-web/webservices/v2).



Dashboard Features

  • Energy Demand Forecasts Over Time
  • Energy Demand Forecasts by Region and Weather Conditions
  • Distribution of Energy Demand Forecasts
  • User input form for predicting energy consumption

I will be using Python and popular data science libraries such as Pandas, Numpy, and Scikit-learn to build the prediction model and Flask to create the web application.
